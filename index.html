Abhinaw Kumar

Currently, I am working as a postdoctoral research fellow in the Mittal group at Texas A&M University. Previously, I worked as a postdoctoral fellow in the Thirumalai group at the University of Texas at Austin. I completed my Ph.D. under the guidance of Prof. Valeria Molinero in 2019 at the University of Utah. My graduate research focused on Statistical Mechanics, Self-Assembly, Porous Materials, and Nucleation.

Research Topics

  • Liquid Crystalline Mesophases from Spherical Particles
  • Nucleation of Gyroid from Disorder
  • Temperature tunes the mechanism of zeolite formation
  • Pore formation in lipid bilayers
